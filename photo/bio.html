BIO

Kristina, born and raised in Laval, Quebec is a young-spirited 21 year-old who graduated from Professional Photography after three years at Dawson College, Montreal in June 2012. She has been collaborating with professionals like models, make-up artists, hair-stylists, and stylists from Model Mayhem since 2009. You can find her profile here. Kristina is currently living in Ottawa, Onario where she is broadening her studies in Interactive Multimedia at Algonquin College where she may combine these new techniques with her passion for photography.
